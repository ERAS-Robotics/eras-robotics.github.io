Venue

The conference venue is located in Worcester, MA, USA, "the Heart of the Commonwealth," a center of higher education that is home to eight colleges and universities, including Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). WPI is known for its strong emphasis on combining theory with hands-on, project-based learning, as well as being home to the first Robotics Department in the United States.

ERAS25 is being held at the Odeum in the Rubin Campus Center of WPI.

Travel

The WPI campus is located 40 miles west of Boston, and can be accessed from Worcester Regional Airport, Commuter Rail from Boston, or coach service from Boston's Logan Airport.
