Final Paper Preparation

When preparing your camera-ready paper, please use the IEEE Template.

Do not insert header, footer, and page numbers.

Please double-check the completeness and correctness of the authors’ names, affiliations, and the corresponding author’s email address.

If the paper has been accepted as a regular paper, the length of the final paper including references must be strictly between 6 (minimum) and 8 pages (maximum).

If the paper has been accepted as a short paper, the length of the final paper including references must be strictly between 2 (minimum) and 4 pages (maximum).

Final Paper Submission

Authors must submit their final paper through IEEE PDF eXpress until April 14th, 2025, 23:59 AOE, using the details as follows:

  • URL: https://ieee-pdf-express.org
  • Conference ID: 63351X

When the paper status is ‘Pass’, then please click on the ‘Approve’ button in the Action field. Then the status will be changed to ‘Approved’.

By submitting your paper, you accept IEEE Terms and Policy.

If the paper is not compliant with IEEE format and requirements or not submitted by the deadline, it will not be included in IEEE Xplore.

Corresponding Author’s Check-list

The corresponding author needs to:

  • Upload the camera-ready paper to the PDF eXpress platform so its status is flagged as ‘Approved’ in the system. For more information, please read the sections above.
  • Complete the registration process, including paying the fees. More information is available at Registration.
  • Complete the copyright transfer through the IEEE eCF platform. An automatic email with the link will be sent to the corresponding author once the author has been registered.

Please note: Non-presented papers will be excluded from the proceedings on IEEE Xplore.
